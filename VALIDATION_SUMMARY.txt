WHAT GETS VALIDATED
-------------------

For EACH of the 56 BBB business models:

STAGE 1: Truth Verification (6 checks per business)
   • Revenue reality (no $500K/mo hallucinations)
   • Cost validation (realistic startup costs)
   • Automation feasibility (100% automation flagged)
   • Timeline realism (no "get rich quick" schemes)
   • Pseudo-science detection (block fake tech)
   • Hallucination patterns (detect AI BS)

STAGE 2: Algorithm Reality Check
   • All algorithms verified as real
   • No "magic AI" claims
   • Concrete tools/platforms required

STAGE 3: Market Reality Check
   • Target market specificity
   • Revenue streams concrete
   • Tools/platforms named

STAGE 4: Parliament Review (if available)
   • ECH0 Prime optimization
   • Semantic lattice analysis
   • Parallel pathways exploration
   • Final approval vote (85%+ threshold)

TOTAL: 336+ validation checks across all businesses

REALITY BOUNDS ENFORCED
-----------------------

Revenue:
  • Max: $100,000/month (hard limit)
  • Reasonable: $50,000/month for automation
  • Cross-check: High automation rarely >$30K/mo
  • Cross-check: Monthly ROI >500% is unrealistic

Startup Cost:
  • Max: $100,000 (beyond requires institutional funding)
  • Must align with complexity/difficulty

Automation:
  • Realistic max: 95% (100% is nearly impossible)
  • Cross-check: 90%+ automation with 10+ hrs/week is contradictory

Timeline:
  • Reasonable: 1-18 months to profitability
  • Max: 36 months (beyond is questionable)
  • Cross-check: Hard difficulty in <3 months is unrealistic

-----------------------------

  Overall Confidence =
    truth_verification_score × 0.25 +
    algorithm_validity_score × 0.15 +
    market_reality_score     × 0.15 +
    parliament_score         × 0.45

  Weighting Rationale:
    • Parliament: 45% (heaviest - includes novelty, viability, safety)
    • Truth Verification: 25% (core reality checks)
    • Algorithm Validity: 15% (ensures real tech)
    • Market Reality: 15% (validates market/revenue)

Per-Business Confidence (WITHOUT Parliament):
  Overall Confidence = (truth + algo + market) / 3

Status Thresholds (UPDATED):
  • VERIFIED: ≥85% overall confidence
  • ACCEPTABLE_WITH_WARNINGS: 70-84% overall confidence
  • FAILED_VALIDATION: <70% overall confidence

Overall Library Health:
  Health = verified_count / total_businesses × 100%

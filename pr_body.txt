💡 What: Replaced two `sum()` generators in `_calculate_confidence` with a single O(N) loop to calculate `top_10_priority` and `total_priority` simultaneously.
🎯 Why: Iterating over the `features` list twice with list slices and generator expressions is redundant overhead. Computing both aggregates in a single pass reduces operations from O(2N) to O(N).
📊 Impact: Eliminates an unnecessary array slice and prevents a second list iteration, offering a micro-optimization that reduces CPU overhead for confidence score computation.
🔬 Measurement: All quantum stack optimizer tests pass with functionally equivalent logic. Benchmarking typical O(N) loops vs duplicate generator expressions demonstrates measurable relative improvement on larger sets.

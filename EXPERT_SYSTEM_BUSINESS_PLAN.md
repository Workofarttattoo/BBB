## Executive Summary

Multi-Domain Expert System offering specialized AI consultation across chemistry, biology, physics, materials science, and business domains. Powered by advanced RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation), ensemble intelligence, and continuous learning capabilities.

## Product Overview

**Core Technology:**
- Vector RAG with ChromaDB/FAISS
- Multi-expert ensemble with voting
- Automatic specialization & fine-tuning
- 15+ domain experts (extensible)
- Integration with autonomous business agents

**Key Differentiators:**
- Ensemble consensus from multiple experts
- Continuous learning from feedback
- Domain-specific fine-tuning
- Production-ready with persistent storage
- Seamless business process integration

## Risk Mitigation

### Technical Risks
- **Risk:** ChromaDB/FAISS failures
- **Mitigation:** Multi-region redundancy, automatic failover

- **Risk:** Model hallucinations
- **Mitigation:** Ensemble voting, confidence thresholds, human review for low confidence

- **Risk:** Scalability issues
- **Mitigation:** Load testing, auto-scaling, CDN for static assets

### Business Risks
- **Risk:** Price too high
- **Mitigation:** Freemium tier, flexible pricing, ROI calculators

- **Risk:** Competition from OpenAI/Anthropic
- **Mitigation:** Focus on niche domains, fine-tuning, enterprise features

- **Risk:** Slow adoption
- **Mitigation:** Pilot program, case studies, money-back guarantee

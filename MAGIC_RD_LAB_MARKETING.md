## Pricing Psychology

### Why These Numbers Work

**$299 (Few Hours)**
- Under $300 psychological threshold
- No approval needed for most managers
- Low enough to "just try it"
- 10x cheaper than alternatives

**$1,000 (Full Day)**
- Round number, easy to remember
- Saves $197 vs hourly rate (good deal!)
- Sweet spot for serious projects
- Standard consulting day rate

**$5,000 (Full Week)**
- Saves $2,000 vs daily rate (huge savings!)
- Still cheaper than hiring one engineer
- Perfect for monthly R&D budgets
- High enough to feel premium

### Upsell Path

1. **Start:** Few Hours ($299) - "Just testing"
2. **Upgrade:** Full Day ($1,000) - "This works!"
3. **Scale:** Full Week ($5,000) - "We need this regularly"
4. **Enterprise:** Custom pricing - "Dedicated access"

**Target:** 40% upgrade to next tier within 3 months
